Who are EDG?

Edward Gaming (EDG) was founded in September 2013 by SanShao, BingGui, and Edward Zhu. EDG is a Chinese ran E-sports orginization competing in many esports such as VALORANT, League of Legends, Hearthstone, and Clash Royale. The VALORANT team won the biggest tournament of the year "Champions 2024" and thats what this website is about.
